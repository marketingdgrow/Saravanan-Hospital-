FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

From what age can I bring my child to the paediatric clinic? +

From day one. We see children from birth through 18 years.

How do I know if my child's development is on track? +

Our Growth & Development Clinic assesses physical, speech, and cognitive milestones. If you have a concern, come in early — don't wait.

My child keeps getting coughs and colds — is that normal? +

Young children average 6–8 viral infections a year. It's usually normal, but warrants a check if infections are severe, frequent, or chest-related.

How is childhood asthma diagnosed? +

Nighttime cough, reduced activity, and frequent chest infections are often the first signs. Spirometry and clinical assessment confirm the diagnosis.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in for a sick child? +

Walk-ins welcome for acute illness. For developmental or growth assessments, booking ahead gives your child the full time they need.
